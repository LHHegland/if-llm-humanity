patterns documented throughout this knowledge system. It is organized into four
clusters: experts in reasoning and behavior (logicians, philosophers, ethicists,
psychologists, economists); experts in communication (linguists, rhetoricians,
conflict mediators, journalists); experts in application (social workers, public
health professionals, educators, community organizers); and experts in history
(historians, anthropologists, archaeologists, archivists).

asks "who studies this?" or "where does this knowledge come from?"
